Reproducibility Checklist

  • Face matches your persona (compare side-by-side)
  • Headline is spelled correctly (Nano Banana drifts)
  • Face is on the right third, not centered or left
  • Main concept object is clearly visible and centered
  • Red arrow and yellow warning triangle are present
  • Text is readable at 320×180 (actually scale it down!)
  • No watermarks, brand names, or ghost faces in background
💡

Quick Tips

🔴 Red + Yellow are non-negotiable. Blue bg, red arrows, yellow warning triangle, yellow key-word text. A/B tested across every major channel.
👤 The face reference controls everything. If Nano Banana generates a different person, re-shoot: good light, 3/4 or front-facing, ≥1024×1024.
📐 One hero object only. "A foot + glucometer" works. Complex scenes don't — 320px on a phone screen.
🔤 Max 4 words on the main headline. Trust the curiosity gap — the full story lives in the video.
